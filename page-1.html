About Me

My name is Benji Wason, and I am an AmSAT certified Alexander Technique teacher in Ridgewood, Queens. I graduated from The Dimon Institute, the world-renowned center for the study of the Alexander Technique and Psycho-Physical education, in 2022. I am also a professional musician who has used the Technique both in overcoming chronic pain and achieving a more effortless and mindful approach to my instrument.

I provide private Alexander Technique lessons to anyone who wants to learn how to undo harmful habits in the way they use their bodies and achieve more effortlessness and balance in their craft and daily life. I teach both online and in-person, with a focus on developing student independence through the mastery of fundamental principles and self-study skills.
